Programs for every
member of your family.

Five programs designed to nurture Tamil language, arts, and community spirit across all age groups.

🏫
தமிழ் பள்ளி
Tamil School

Weekly Tamil language, culture, and heritage classes for children of all ages — the heart of our community.

Visit School Site → 📚
