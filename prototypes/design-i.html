எங்கள் திட்டங்கள் — Our Programs

Programs of the Society

Five pillars of the community.

Like the columns of a mandapa — each pillar distinct, all supporting the same sacred space.

🏫

தமிழ்ப் பள்ளி

Tamil School

Classical language education every Sunday. K through high school, taught by dedicated community volunteers.
📚

நூலகம்

Library

Tamil books, classical texts, and children's literature. Borrow, read, and reconnect with home.
🎨

பட்டறை

Pattarai

Arts studio for Tamil creative expression — Bharatanatyam, kolam, painting, and more.
🎵

இசை மன்றம்

Music Club

Carnatic tradition — mridangam, veena, vocal. Weekly practice and annual kutcheri performances.
🤝

தன்னார்வல

Volunteer

Community service — the Tamil value of upliftment made visible every year in Wisconsin.
