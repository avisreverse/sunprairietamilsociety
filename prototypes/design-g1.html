In 2012, Tamil families in Sun Prairie, Wisconsin gathered around one question: how do we give our children the language and the culture we carry in our hearts? The answer was this — five programs, one community, twelve years of roots.

New to Wisconsin?
You are home.

Whether you arrived last month or fifteen years ago, SPTS is your community. Our board volunteers their time so no Tamil family faces the desert crossing alone.
