Our Programs · எங்கள் திட்டங்கள்

Five Ways We Keep Tamil Culture Alive

Each program is a thread in the fabric of our community — woven together across generations, borders, and seasons.

Program 01 · தமிழ் பள்ளி

01

தமிழ்ப் பள்ளி

Tamil Language School

Every Sunday, children from kindergarten through high school gather to learn the oldest living classical language in the world. We teach reading, writing, speaking, and the literature that has endured 2,000 years.

Enroll your child →

Program 02 · நூலகம்

02

நூலகம்

Tamil Library

Our community library holds Tamil books, classical texts, contemporary fiction, and children's literature. Every borrowed book is a conversation with the generations who came before.

Browse the collection →

Program 03 · பட்டறை

03

பட்டறை

Pattarai Creative Studio

Tamil arts, crafts, and creative expression in a studio setting. Kolam workshops, Bharatanatyam, painting, storytelling — Pattarai is where culture becomes creation.

Join a workshop →
🎨

Program 04 · இசை மன்றம்

04

இசை மன்றம்

Carnatic Music Club

From mridangam to veena, from beginner sruti to seasoned kutcheri — our music club brings the Carnatic tradition to life in Wisconsin through weekly practice sessions and annual concerts.
